About

The Cook County Justice Resource Map was created in partnership between the Cook County ACT Court, Chicago Appleseed Fund for Justice, the Data in Public Policy student group at the University of Chicago Harris School, the Cook County Adult Probation Department, and TASC.

This is an adaptation of Derek Eder's Searchable Map Template using Google Fusion Tables.

Disclaimer: This resource map is in a beta testing mode and may contain incomplete or inaccurate information. For the most current information about treatment services and health insurance coverage, please contact the treatment provider or your health insurer directly.
